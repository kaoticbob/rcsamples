American Dream Slot Machine - Version Uno

Experience Designer, Game Writer, Lead Programmer, Object Designer

A sample of slot machine gameplay with citizenship test designed to interrupt regular flow of a slot machine. This is Karimi's first attempt to create a slot machine to reduce gambling addiction. Karimi began this quest to be in dialogue with his parents' gambling addiction which at times led to them losing hundreds of thousands, and left Karimi stuck on the outside of the casino waiting for his parents for hours. Rather than focus on the isolation part, Karimi focused on stumping players with the US Citizenship Test, and designed icons to serve as a metaphor for the gambling aspect of the mythological concept of the American Dream.
