“Artivism For The Love Of Art And Community,” co-written with Carlos Salomon

Please note: These are not the final published essays, as I created PDFs from the final proofs before the galleys were made.

A peer reviewed chapter about the role of activism in artistic practice. Salomon and I traverse history and various artists to explore the symbiotic nature between activism and artistic practice. “Artivism For The Love Of Art And Community,” co-written with Carlos Salomon, will be featured in Introduction to Comparative Ethnic Studies: Decolonial Love, Knowledge and Revolution (Routledge 2025), ed. Nicholas Baham, Jocyl Sacramento, Carlos Manuel Salomon, Jennifer Tran, Anndretta Wilson
